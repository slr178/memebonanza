How It Works

🎰 Game Mechanics

🎯 Win Condition

Get 3+ consecutive columns with the same symbol starting from the leftmost column to win! The more consecutive columns you match, the bigger your payout.

💰 Payout System

Payouts are calculated as a percentage of the prize pool. Rarer symbols give bigger rewards! Each symbol has different rarity and payout rates.

🔄 Cascade System

When you win, those symbols explode and disappear. New symbols drop down to fill the gaps. Chain multiple wins for multiplied payouts!

⚡ Buildup Effects

Every spin features a 3-second dramatic countdown with screen shake, blur effects, and neon pulses for maximum excitement and suspense!
